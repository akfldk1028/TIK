Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to publish content on your behalf:

  • TikTok user ID and access token (via OAuth)
  • Basic profile information (username)

2. How We Use Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Authenticate with the TikTok API
  • Publish video content to your TikTok account

3. Data Storage

Access tokens are stored securely in environment variables on private servers. We do not store tokens in databases or share them with third parties.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

5. Data Retention

We retain your access tokens only as long as needed to provide the Service. You may request deletion at any time.

6. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including encrypted storage and secure API communication.

7. Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to your stored data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke access via TikTok's app settings

8. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

9. Contact
